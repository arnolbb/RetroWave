# Product Strategy - RetroWave

## Register
product

## Purpose
An interactive vaporwave arcade experience featuring a retro Space Invaders game and a synchronized synthwave music player.

## Target Users
- Gamers seeking a quick nostalgic arcade experience.
- Vaporwave/Synthwave enthusiasts looking for a visually relaxing workspace tool.
- Developers looking for interactive web-based canvas showcases.

## Brand Personality
- Nostalgic & Retro-Futuristic
- Neon & High-Contrast
- Interactive & Responsive
- High-fidelity visual feedback

## Anti-References
- Plain corporate grey dashboards
- Flat, dull, low-contrast layouts
- Static non-animated designs

## Strategic Design Principles
1. **Juicy Feedback**: Every button press, hit, and state change must have satisfying visual or audio feedback.
2. **High Readability**: Neon elements must contrast sharply against dark/tinted backgrounds to ensure accessibility.
3. **Robust Game Loop**: High performance canvas-based updates to guarantee 60fps gameplay.
